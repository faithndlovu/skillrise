## Logo
- `hopehub.png` — the original logo (solid dark background).
- `logo.png` — the same logo with the dark background made **transparent**
  (generated from `hopehub.png`). This is the one used on the site: it
  appears in the nav bar, the footers, and as the browser-tab favicon
  (referenced everywhere as `images/logo.png`).

Because the logo contains both light and dark elements, the lighter parts
are faint on light backgrounds and the darker parts are faint on dark
backgrounds. If you ever get a single-colour (e.g. all-white) version of
the mark, it will sit even more cleanly in the dark footer.
