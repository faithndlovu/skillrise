## Logo
- `logo-source.png` — the original logo artwork (black wordmark on a white
  background).
- `logo.png` — the same logo with the white background made **transparent**
  (generated from `logo-source.png`). This is the one used on the site:
  nav bar, footers, and the browser-tab favicon (referenced everywhere as
  `images/logo.png`).

The wordmark is dark, so on the dark footer the logo is shown on a small
white card (see `.footer-logo-img` in `css/styles.css`); on the light nav
bar it sits directly on the background.
